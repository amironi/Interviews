
Use IDA and Ollydbg to complete this exercise.

1. Patch Minesweeper such that it doesn't play any sounds, ever.

2. Patch Minesweeper such that the recorded score is always 1 second, regardless of level or actual result.

3. Patch Minesweeper such that all mines are always revealed. (This might require deep analysis of how board cells are represented. See http://www.codeproject.com/KB/trace/minememoryreader.aspx for interesting details.)
